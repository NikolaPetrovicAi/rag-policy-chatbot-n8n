Shipping, Customs & Restricted Items Policy
1. Purpose and Scope

This policy defines the rules governing shipping, delivery, customs handling, and restrictions related to specific products and destinations. It applies to all physical products sold by the Store and should be read in conjunction with the Store Policy Overview.

2. Shipping Regions and Availability

The Store ships to selected international destinations. Shipping availability may vary based on:

destination country or region,

carrier limitations,

product type,

regulatory or safety restrictions.

The Store does not guarantee shipping availability to all countries at all times. Certain destinations may be temporarily or permanently excluded.

3. Order Processing and Dispatch

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 Business Days after payment confirmation, unless otherwise stated on the product page.

Orders are not processed or dispatched on weekends or public holidays. During periods of high order volume, processing times may be extended.

An order is considered dispatched once it has been handed over to the shipping carrier.

4. Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery timeframes are provided at checkout and are calculated from the dispatch date, not the order date.

Delivery times are estimates only and may be affected by:

carrier delays,

customs inspections,

weather conditions,

transportation disruptions,

incorrect or incomplete shipping information.

The Store does not guarantee delivery by a specific date.

5. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, VAT, or similar charges imposed by the destination country.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise:

shipments are sent on a DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid) basis,

the Customer is responsible for all customs-related charges,

the Store does not reimburse customs duties or taxes.

Failure to pay customs charges may result in shipment delays, returns, or abandonment.

6. Customs Delays and Refused Shipments

The Store is not responsible for delays caused by customs authorities.

If a shipment is refused by the Customer due to unpaid duties or regulatory issues:

the order may be returned to the Store or abandoned by the carrier,

return shipping costs and customs fees may be deducted from any refund,

refunds are not guaranteed for abandoned shipments.

7. Restricted and Prohibited Items

Certain products may be restricted or prohibited in specific regions due to safety, regulatory, or carrier requirements.

Restricted items may include, but are not limited to:

lithium battery–powered devices,

wireless communication equipment,

high-capacity power banks,

products requiring local certifications or approvals.

It is the Customer’s responsibility to verify local import regulations before placing an order.

8. Lithium Battery Shipments

Products containing lithium batteries are subject to additional shipping restrictions.

Key conditions:

air shipping may be limited or unavailable to certain regions,

ground shipping alternatives may be required,

delivery times may be longer than standard estimates.

The Store reserves the right to cancel orders containing battery-powered products if shipping restrictions cannot be satisfied.

9. PO Boxes and Special Addresses

The Store does not ship to:

PO boxes,

freight forwarding addresses,

military addresses (APO/FPO/DPO), unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Orders placed with unsupported addresses may be canceled or delayed.

10. Lost Shipments

A shipment is considered lost in transit only after the carrier confirms loss or after a reasonable investigation period.

Lost shipment claims:

must be reported within 14 days of the last tracking update,

may require carrier investigation,

are resolved according to carrier policies.

Resolution may include replacement or refund at the Store’s discretion.

11. Damaged Shipments

If a shipment arrives visibly damaged, the Customer must:

report the damage within 48 hours of delivery,

provide photographic evidence of the packaging and product,

retain all original packaging materials.

Claims submitted outside this timeframe may be denied.

12. Incorrect or Incomplete Shipping Information

The Customer is responsible for providing accurate and complete shipping details.

The Store is not responsible for:

delays caused by incorrect addresses,

shipments delivered to unintended recipients,

additional fees resulting from address corrections.

Reshipment may require payment of additional shipping costs.

13. Relationship to Other Policies

This policy should be read together with:

Store Policy Overview,

Returns, Exchanges & Restocking Fees Policy,

Warranty & Repairs (RMA) Policy.

In case of conflict, the Store Policy Overview and Terms and Conditions take precedence.
