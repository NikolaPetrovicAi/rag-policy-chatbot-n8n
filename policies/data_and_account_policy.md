Data & Account Policy
1. Purpose and Scope

This policy describes how customer accounts and related data are created, used, retained, restricted, and deleted. It applies to all users who create an account, place an order, or interact with the Store’s services.

This policy should be read together with the Store Policy Overview and other applicable store policies.

2. Account Creation and Use

Customers may create an account to place orders, track shipments, access support, and manage preferences.

Each Customer is responsible for:

maintaining accurate account information,

safeguarding login credentials,

all activity performed through their account.

Accounts must not be used for unlawful activities, fraud, or policy circumvention.

3. Types of Data Collected

The Store may collect and process the following categories of data:

account information (name, email address, login identifiers),

order and transaction history,

shipping and billing details,

support communications and attachments,

technical and security data (IP address, device identifiers, login timestamps).

Payment card data is processed exclusively by third-party payment providers and is not stored by the Store.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Customer data may be processed for the following purposes:

order fulfillment and delivery,

warranty and return handling,

customer support and dispute resolution,

fraud detection and prevention,

compliance with legal and regulatory obligations,

service improvement and security monitoring.

Data will not be used for purposes incompatible with those listed above without a valid legal basis.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose.

Unless otherwise required by law:

order and transaction records are retained for up to 7 years,

support communications are retained for up to 3 years,

inactive account data may be anonymized or deleted after 24 months of inactivity.

Retention periods may be extended in cases involving disputes, chargebacks, or legal obligations.

6. Account Suspension and Restrictions

The Store may suspend or restrict an account in cases of:

suspected fraud or abuse,

repeated chargebacks,

violation of store policies,

security concerns.

During suspension:

order placement may be disabled,

support access may be limited to essential communications,

ongoing cases may be paused pending investigation.

7. Account Termination

Accounts may be terminated:

at the Customer’s request, subject to data retention obligations,

by the Store for serious or repeated policy violations.

Termination does not affect:

outstanding orders,

unresolved disputes,

legal or financial obligations.

8. Data Access, Correction, and Deletion Requests

Depending on applicable law, Customers may request to:

access their personal data,

correct inaccurate information,

request deletion or anonymization of data.

Deletion requests may be denied or delayed if:

data is required for legal compliance,

an active dispute or investigation exists,

retention is required to prevent fraud.

9. Fraud Prevention and Monitoring

The Store implements technical and organizational measures to detect and prevent fraud, including:

transaction monitoring,

account behavior analysis,

identity and risk assessments.

Fraud-related data may be retained longer than standard retention periods where legally permitted.

10. Data Security

The Store applies reasonable security measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Despite these measures, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Policy Conflicts and Legal Rights

In the event of a conflict between this policy and applicable data protection laws, mandatory legal requirements shall prevail.

Where conflicts arise between store policies, the Store Policy Overview determines precedence.
